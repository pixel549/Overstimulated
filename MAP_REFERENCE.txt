║                                                                              ║
║  COORDINATE SYSTEM EXPLAINED:                                               ║
║  - RAW coordinates: Negative values (what you see in code: -269, -233, etc.)║
║  - ACTUAL coordinates: RAW + OFFSET (OFFSET_X=270, OFFSET_Y=233)            ║
║  - PIXEL coordinates: ACTUAL × TILE_SIZE (TILE_SIZE = 20px)                 ║
║                                                                              ║
║  Example: Living Room                                                       ║
║    RAW: x=-241, y=-226                                                      ║
║    ACTUAL: x=29, y=7  (after adding OFFSET_X and OFFSET_Y)                 ║
║    PIXELS: x=580, y=140  (multiply by TILE_SIZE=20)                        ║
║                                                                              ║
║  WHEN MOVING THINGS:                                                        ║
║  - Positive X = Move RIGHT                                                  ║
║  - Negative X = Move LEFT                                                   ║
║  - Positive Y = Move DOWN                                                   ║
║  - Negative Y = Move UP                                                     ║

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                         QUICK REFERENCE CONVERSIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

To convert RAW → ACTUAL:      actual_x = raw_x + 270
                              actual_y = raw_y + 233

To convert ACTUAL → PIXELS:   pixel_x = actual_x × 20
                              pixel_y = actual_y × 20

To convert RAW → PIXELS:      pixel_x = (raw_x + 270) × 20
                              pixel_y = (raw_y + 233) × 20


EXAMPLE: Moving something 5 tiles to the RIGHT:
  - In RAW coords: change x from -241 to -236  (add +5)
  - In ACTUAL coords: change x from 29 to 34   (add +5)
  - In PIXELS: change x from 580 to 680        (add +100)

EXAMPLE: Moving something 3 tiles UP:
  - In RAW coords: change y from -226 to -229  (subtract -3, more negative)
  - In ACTUAL coords: change y from 7 to 4     (subtract -3)
  - In PIXELS: change y from 140 to 80         (subtract -60)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                              USAGE TIPS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. When discussing coordinates, specify which system you're using:
   "Move it to RAW (-240, -220)" or "Move it to PIXEL (600, 260)"

2. The code uses RAW coordinates internally (negative values)

3. For small adjustments, think in tiles:
   - 1 tile = 20 pixels
   - "+5 tiles right" = add +5 to RAW x-coord
   - "-3 tiles up" = subtract 3 from RAW y-coord (more negative)

4. Remember: More negative Y = HIGHER on screen (towards top)
            More negative X = FURTHER LEFT on screen

5. Living room is the central hub - most paths go through it

6. Dog Patio is at the bottom of the house (largest Y values)
